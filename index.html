About Me

Hi! My name is Daryl Devon Umali Bacurin. I enjoy programming, which copes with my self-deprecating habits. My interest in programming started when I entered college as a Computer Science student. With little to no programming knowledge, I found myself entranced by the fact that programming languages can be used to communicate with a computer to do things for me.

The spark of enlightenment makes me imagine things that a programming language can accomplish. With that realization, I embark on a journey to learn and seek knowledge to help mankind! - No, I'm kidding. I only want to earn money like your average joe, have a peaceful life, a family of my own and learn programming languages.

Currently, I'm training under the care of Kodego boot camp to learn skills to become a full-stack developer.
